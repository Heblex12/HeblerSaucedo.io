Game Design

The purpose of this project was to create a game for children that was both fun and educational. The game was for the "Seattle Pacific Science Center" and had to follow the theme of astronomy. So we created our team name "UISIA".

In the begining we had no idea what to make as a game, but we continue we though of billards and space. we came up with the of a AR billard game as each ball represented each planet in our solar system.

La Frontera

Our Journey

In this project our goal was to create a voice for immigrants from around the world in the United States.We decided to create a website which would host interactive stories of immigrants. In this stories we would give the reader the ability to be in the shoes of the immigrants by having them make choice the immigrant had to make. He will get to better understand why the immigrant made the choice to come to the United States. Through we hope to create Empathy and understanding, hopefully create a better enviroment for immigrants here in the United States.

Why Immigration?

"The United States experienced major waves of immigration during the colonial era, the first part of the 19th century and from the 1880s to 1920" - History.com

MIGRATION is one thing but ACCEPTANCE and ADAPTING is another.
